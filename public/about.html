About FoodCheck 🥗

The Problem

Most people struggle to understand what is actually in the packaged food they buy. Nutrition labels are small and confusing, making it difficult to compare labels across products in a store aisle.

Stakeholders

FoodCheck is designed for anyone who wants to make smarter food decisions. It is useful for health-conscious consumers and people managing dietary restrictions such as allergies, diabetes, or high cholesterol.

The Solution

FoodCheck allows users to enter a product barcode and instantly receive a full nutrition breakdown including calories, sugar, fat, protein, allergens, and a Nutri-Score grade.

API Used

Open Food Facts is a free, open-source food product database. FoodCheck sends barcodes to this API to retrieve nutrition information directly from their database.

Nutri-Score

The Nutri-Score is a system that grades the nutrition of a product on a scale of A to E. It is based on the amount of healthy and unhealthy nutrients.

Negative points: energy, saturated fat, sugars, sodium (high levels are considered unhealthy).
Positive points: the proportion of fruits, vegetables and nuts, of olive, colza and nut oils, of fibers and proteins.


Explanation from Open Food Facts: https://world.openfoodfacts.org/nutriscore
